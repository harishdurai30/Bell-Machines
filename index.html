Workflow Process

How the BPM CTM 526 works

Five precise steps from carton loading to continuous output — engineered for maximum throughput with zero compromise on quality.

1

Load Carton

Place the carton onto the machine work bench at the set working height for effortless loading by the operator.

2

Align & Clamp

Pneumatic air-operated clamp plate automatically adjusts to the carton height, holding it securely in place.

3

Precision Taping

Operator activates via press button or foot switch — the machine applies tape with precise tension and alignment.

4

Secure Sealing

5

Continuous Output

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about the BPM CTM 526? We've answered the most common ones below.

Can the machine handle heavy cartons?

Yes. The BPM CTM 526 is built with an industrial-grade heavy duty frame and robust mechanical components that can handle cartons of varying weights. The pneumatic clamp system adjusts to securely hold cartons of different heights and weights during the taping process. For specific weight requirements, contact our technical team for a custom specification.

Is the taping width customizable?

Absolutely. Both the tape width and strapping size (minimum and maximum) are fully customizable per your requirements. When placing your order, simply specify your carton dimensions and tape width needs, and we engineer the machine to your exact specifications. This makes the BPM CTM 526 ideal for any packaging application.

Can it run continuously in a multi-shift environment?

Yes. The BPM CTM 526 is specifically designed for continuous 24/7 industrial operation. All internal components — pneumatic, mechanical, and electrical — are sourced from premium industrial-grade suppliers to ensure reliability and durability across multi-shift production environments. The machine is ideal for facilities running.

Is installation and commissioning support available?

Yes. Bell Machines provides complete installation and commissioning support for every machine we supply. Our experienced technical team will visit your facility, install the machine, commission it to ensure optimal performance, and provide operator training. We also offer ongoing after-sales technical support via phone and on-site visits when required.

Can it integrate with an existing automated packing line?

Yes. The BPM CTM 526 can be fitted with a PLC module at additional cost, enabling repetitive automated taping for integration with existing production and packing lines. This makes it suitable for fully automated packaging workflows where manual intervention is minimized. Contact us to discuss your automation requirements in detail.
